The Best Bullies in The Burgh

At Cribz Kennels Bullies we are passionate about breeding exceptional XL American Bullies. Based in Pittsburgh, PA and founded in 2020 by owner and breeder, Raheel Asghar, our commitment to quality and excellence has allowed us to share our beloved pups with families around the globe. We take pride in our dogs' top pedigree, impressive size, and outstanding health, behavior, and temperament. Each dog is raised with love and care, ensuring they become the perfect companions. Discover the Cribz difference—where quality meets love!
