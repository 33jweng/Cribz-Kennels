About Our Wonderful Breed

Pure Breeds, Pure Joy

American Bullies

The American Bully is a loyal, confident, and affectionate breed that was originally developed in the United States as a companion dog. Known for their muscular build, strong stature, and broad heads, American Bullies are athletic yet gentle, making them excellent family pets. They have an easygoing nature, and despite their powerful appearance, they are incredibly friendly and great with children. The breed comes in a variety of sizes, from Pocket to XL, all of which maintain the same admirable temperament and strong physical traits. Their short, glossy coats are low-maintenance, making them an appealing choice for those seeking a combination of beauty and ease of care.

XL American Bullies

The XL American Bully is the largest variety of the breed, standing between 20 to 23 inches tall at the shoulders and weighing 80 to 150 pounds or more. Despite their larger size, XL American Bullies are known for their calm and balanced temperament, often described as gentle giants. They are affectionate and protective, making them excellent family companions who are both loving and loyal. Their imposing appearance—characterized by a broad chest, powerful stance, and muscular frame—belies their sweet and friendly nature. XL Bullies are especially prized for their combination of size, strength, and their nurturing, family-friendly disposition.
